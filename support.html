Support Lab

The Computer Science Club runs a semesterly support lab to help students needing some extra help and guidance in lower level classes with their assignments and topics. All the hours, and the current status, of the support lab can be found on this page.

Winter 2017

The support lab is now open for the Winter 2017 semester. Drop by OM1365 during one of the scheduled sessions, or email us at supportlab@trucsclub.ca.

Know your stuff? Volunteer with the Support Lab!

The Winter 2017 call for volunteers is now open! Each semester, students depend on the support lab for help with lower level courses. The entire operation is volunteer run, so we need students like you to help out! If you're a strong computing student willing to share your knowledge, fill out the form below to get started!
